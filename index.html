MoodFuse

What is MoodFuse?

MoodFuse is an app that gives you recommendations for movies, books, and drinks based on the your mood, as indicated by an emoji that you select.

Simply choose an emoji based on your current mood

   

And we will provide movie, book, and drink recommendations
